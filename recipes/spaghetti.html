SPAGHETTI!!!!

Learn how to cook delicious pasta for laura so she can mash mash mash

Ingredients:
  • Your favorite spaghetti
  • IKEA Meatballs
  • Your favorite red sauce
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Laura
  • Broccoli
Steps:
  1. Boil water in pot big enough for pasta
  2. Add pasta to boiled water. Cook until slightly al dente. You want it to finish cooking in the sauce
  3. While this is happening, heat up sauce and meatballs and steam vegetables.
  4. Strain pasta using pasta strainer. If you want keep a bit of the starchy water
  5. Put pasta and starchy water back in pot. Mix in Sauce, Meatballs, and Vegetables.
  6. Add Salt, Pepper, and Parmesan for taste. Enjoy!
